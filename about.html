About Wame's Cuddle Crew

Wame's Cuddle Crew is a friendly pet adoption center dedicated to helping animals find loving homes.
We believe every pet deserves love, care, and a place to call home.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, caring environment for pets while connecting them with responsible, loving families.

Our Volunteers

Our amazing volunteers make everything possible - from feeding and caring for pets to helping new pet owners settle in.
We love our team and the work they do!

Our Values

  • Compassion for all animals
  • Community engagement
  • Responsibility and care
  • Transparency and trust

Meet Our Amazing Volunteer Team

Our dedicated volunteers make Wame's Cuddle Crew possible.
Thank you to everyone who helps care for our furry friends!
